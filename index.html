Scuba Diver Adventure Quest: Finding Atlantis

Let your adventure begin!
















You are a world famous scuba diver and you believe that you have found the location of the lost city of Atlantis. Unfortunately, entering Atlantis is tricky. There are traps and obstacles set up millenia ago to be overcome. The creatures of the deep guard this sacred place. Do you have what it takes to be the one to rediscover Atlantis? Enter different parts of the map and make your decisions, you can reenter as many times as you need to ensure you make the right choice and incrase your progress (seen in the top right). Be careful though, the wrong selections will reduce your health! Begin your adventure now!
